MAP672: Programming for Web Mapping

Web Cartography at the University of Kentucky

Book Deserts in St. Tammany Parish

This quantitative thematic map of St. Tammany Parish in southeast Louisiana plots libraries per 5,000 people in an effort to identify potential "book deserts" in the region.

The map was created using QGIS.

  • See the map: Book Deserts in St. Tammany Parish
  • Data source: United States Census Bureau

Cancer Mortality Rates in the USA

This quantitative thematic map of America shows cancer rates for all cancer types by mortality, for all races, sexes, and ages, averaged over the years 2008 - 2012.

The map was created using QGIS and the web-mapping platform Carto.

  • See the map: Cancer Mortality Rates in the USA
  • Data source: National Cancer Institute

US Electric Power Generation

This proportional symbol map of America shows power generation for hydro, nuclear, and solar power plants in America.

The map was created with custom JavaScript code using the software Brackets.

  • See the map: US Electric Power Generation
  • Data source: US Energy Information Administration
